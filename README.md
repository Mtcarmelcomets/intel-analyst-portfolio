# Intel Analyst Portfolio

**12-Week AI-CIA Intelligence Analyst Career Transition Program**

A comprehensive, self-directed study portfolio demonstrating proficiency in Python programming, machine learning, natural language processing, geospatial analysis, and intelligence tradecraft  built to meet Intelligence Community Directive (ICD) 203 Analytic Standards.

## About This Program

- **Duration:** 12 weeks (60 weekday sessions)
- - **Schedule:** 6:30 - 8:15 PM MDT (105 min/day)
  - - **Total Study Hours:** 105 hours
    - - **Start Date:** April 27, 2026
      - - **End Date:** July 17, 2026
       
        - ## Weekly Themes
       
        - | Week | Dates | Theme |
        - |------|-------|-------|
        - | 1 | Apr 27 - May 1 | Python Foundations & Global Economics |
        - | 2 | May 4 - 8 | Pandas Data Wrangling & Migration Intelligence |
        - | 3 | May 11 - 15 | Data Visualization & Terrorism Analysis |
        - | 4 | May 18 - 22 | Statistics for Analysts & Cyber Threat Intel |
        - | 5 | May 25 - 29 | Intro to ML & Arms/Military Analysis |
        - | 6 | Jun 1 - 5 | Classification Models & AI/Tech Competition |
        - | 7 | Jun 8 - 12 | NLP & Climate/Energy Security |
        - | 8 | Jun 15 - 19 | Clustering & Political Instability |
        - | 9 | Jun 22 - 26 | Geospatial Intelligence & Middle East Focus |

        - ## Repository Structure
       
        - ```
          intel-analyst-portfolio/

          ## Tools & Technologies

          - **Programming:** Python 3.x
          - - **Data Wrangling:** Pandas, NumPy
            - - **Visualization:** Matplotlib, Seaborn, Plotly, Folium
              - - **Machine Learning:** scikit-learn
                - - **NLP:** NLTK, spaCy, Hugging Face Transformers, Gensim
                  - - **Geospatial:** GeoPandas, Folium
                    - - **Time Series:** statsmodels (ARIMA, VAR), Facebook Prophet
                      - - **Network Analysis:** NetworkX
                        - - **Version Control:** Git, GitHub

                                              - ## Intelligence Standards
                                             
                                              - All assessments in this portfolio adhere to [ICD 203 Analytic Standards](https://www.dni.gov/files/documents/ICD/ICD%20203%20Analytic%20Standards.pdf), including:
                                              - - Properly sourced and caveated analysis
                                                - - Confidence level assessments (low, moderate, high)
                                                  - - Consideration of alternative hypotheses
                                                    - - Distinction between evidence and judgment
